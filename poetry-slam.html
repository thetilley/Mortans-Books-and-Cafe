Mortan's Poetry Slam

On April the 3rd between 18:00 - 22:00 the Poetry Slam will take place. Here we will see the best poets in Bristol go head to head to see who is the winner. Voted by the audance, the winner will recieve a £25 gift card to use at Mortan's Books and Cafe. 2nd will be awarded £10 and 3rd a free drink of there choice. But more Imprtantly then the winnings, everyone (no matter there place) will reisve the reconition that they deserve in a fun and non judging atmosphire. Everyone that gets on the stage is a winner!
