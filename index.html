Who are we?

Here at Mortan's books and cafe we have made a safe and relaxing place for creatives off all types to meet, collab and most importantly, create! Made from there love of books, art and a nice cuppa, Mortan's is run inderpently by husband and wife Jonas and Martha. They set out to make a place for everyone (no matter what creative background) to meet away from the noisy pub and bar scene. So no matter your passion, wether it be litriture, vintage board games, or just looking for a friendly chat, pop in!
